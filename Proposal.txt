Glossary

SideScroller - a video game in which the gameplay action is viewed from a side-view camera angle, and the onscreen characters generally move from the left side of the screen to the right to meet an objective.
Fps - frames per second
Powerup - objects that instantly benefit or add extra abilities to the game character.
